# How to Pitch "Iteration 1" vs "Iteration 2"

You have two incredibly powerful tools at your disposal tomorrow. Because we pushed Version 1 (V1) to the live internet yesterday, and built Version 2 (V2) entirely on your local machine today, you can present them interchangeably without breaking anything.

Here is exactly how you handle that presentation.

---

### Step 1: The "Live Site" Pitch (Iteration 1)
**What it is:** The foundational sales funnel. It proves you can generate leads and handle modern web design.
**Where it lives:** `https://heytimmy.com` (Your custom domain hosted on GitHub Pages).

**How to present it:**
1. Pull out your phone or tablet and go directly to `heytimmy.com`.
2. Hand the device to Arthur. 
3. *The Script:* "Professor, check this out on your phone. See how fast that loads? Look at the lead form—it's right there. No friction. This is Live Iteration 1. It is already built to convert better than the old Squarespace site."

---

### Step 2: The "Future Expansion" Pitch (Iteration 2)
**What it is:** The premium expansion. It proves you are thinking about the *business* (Events, Seminars, Kicksite CRM, Annual Paid-in-Full cash flow). **Crucially, this version is NOT live on the internet yet.** It is built purely as a private prototype for this meeting.
**Where it lives:** Right here on your Desktop in the `RUFFHOUSE` folder (`file:///Users/tim.v.browne/Desktop/RUFFHOUSE/index.html`). 

**How to present it:**
1. Once Arthur is impressed with V1 on his phone, open your laptop where you have the local files.
2. Open the `Desktop/RUFFHOUSE/index.html` file in your browser to show him the new `Events` tab. Show him `events.html` and the pulsing "New Event" pass for the seminar.
3. *The Script:* "But hitting 'publish' on a website isn't enough. We need to expand the product. I've already wired up the blueprints for Iteration 2 right here on my laptop. We can launch a dedicated Events page to sell the Annual Seminar Pass and feature past Black Belt visits to build our authority online."

---

### Step 3: The Financial Close (The Presentation Deck)
**What it is:** The financial math that proves your value and explains the $215/mo premium switch.
**Where it lives:** `Desktop/RUFFHOUSE/presentation_deck.html`

**How to present it:**
1. Open up `presentation_deck.html` on your laptop in full screen.
2. Use your right and left arrow keys to slide through the pitch.
3. Walk him through the **Kicksite Web-to-Lead integration** (meaning no manual data entry for his brother), the **ACH discount system** to kill the 4% fee, and the **Paid in Full (Annual) discount** to generate immediate $2k+ cash drops to fix the facility. 

By keeping V1 live and showing V2 locally, you prove that you test features before pushing them to the public, establishing yourself as a true professional digital manager.
