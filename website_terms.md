# Ruffhouse Renton Website Terms & Conditions

### 1. Membership & Billing
Memberships are billed monthly via automated recurring payment. A valid payment method must be kept on file at all times.

### 2. Annual Adjustment (COLA)
To maintain facility standards and elite instruction, all memberships are subject to a **3.0% Annual Cost of Living Adjustment**, effective January 1st of each calendar year. This adjustment applies to the base membership rate only and replaces any previous monthly "convenience" or credit card surcharges. Members will be notified 30 days prior to any change.

### 3. Open Mat & Gear Policy
Ruffhouse Renton maintains an "Open Gear" policy. Students may wear any clean, well-maintained Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu Gi or No-Gi attire. For competition safety, we recommend students weigh-in and train in the gear they intend to compete in; mandatory academy patches are not required.

### 4. Cancellation
12-month agreements require 30 days' written notice for cancellation. Month-to-month memberships may be cancelled at any time with 15 days' notice.

### 5. Affiliate Drop-Ins & The Passport
"Passport" members must maintain their "Home Academy" for primary billing but maintain "Open Mat" rights at all participating global affiliates (including Identity BJJ). Standard Foundation and Squad members are subject to standard drop-in rates at affiliate gyms.

### 6. Waiver of Liability
All students must have a signed physical activity waiver on file prior to stepping on the mats. Jiu-Jitsu is a high-contact sport; participation is strictly at the member's own risk.
