Executive Strategy Proposal

Ruffhouse Renton
2026 Initiative

Transitioning from "Legacy Pricing" to a "High-Performance Academy" model. Capturing the Seattle market, eliminating conversion friction, and automating our sales funnel.

02

The Market Reality

  • The Competition: Premium Eastside/Seattle academies (Gracie Barra Kirkland, Seattle BJJ) are charging $175 - $200+ per month, frequently with mandatory gear fees.
  • The Inflation Hit: Since 2019, inflation has devalued our unadjusted rates by nearly 20%. CPI sits at 2.4%; the Social Security COLA was 2.8%.
  • The Friction Point: Our 4% credit card surcharge is a proven conversion killer. Buyers perceive it as a "hidden tax" rather than the cost of business.
  • The Solution ($5 ACH Discount): We build all processing fees into the base price, but offer a $5 monthly discount for ACH/Direct bank transfers. This instantly eliminates CC fees entirely while making the student feel like they won a deal.
  • The Pivot: Move from "cheap base + fees" to a Premium, All-In standard that reflects the Carlson Gracie lineage.
03

The Market Reality (King County)

  • Gracie Barra (Bellevue/Seattle): ~$200 - $250/mo (Strict uniform policies and high enrollment fees).
  • 10th Planet (Seattle/Bellevue): ~$180 - $220/mo (No-Gi only, highly specialized).
  • Summit/Mat Chess: ~$160 - $190/mo (Standard local competitive rates).
  • The Opportunity: We aren't Gracie Barra, and we don't have a pristine million-dollar facility yet. But the instruction is world-class. We position Ruffhouse firmly in the Mid-High Premium Tier ($195/mo)—respecting the Carlson Gracie lineage without overpricing our current physical reality.

The "Pay In Full" Advantage

05

The Premium "Punch List" (Facility)

  • The Reality Check: We cannot charge $195/mo without the facility matching the instruction. The gym currently needs physical TLC.
  • The Paid-in-Full Strategy: We introduce an annual "Paid-in-Full" option at $2,000/yr. Just three students taking this offer generates $6,000 in upfront cash.
  • The Immediate Fixes: That $6k cash injection is ring-fenced strictly for facility repair:
    • → Professional deep cleaning of the entire facility
    • → Fresh interior paint (Ruffhouse Red/Black/White)
    • → Ceiling repairs and lighting upgrades
    • → Complete bathroom overhaul (New toilet, fixtures, luxury soap/towels)
06

The Sales Automation

  • The $99 Tripwire: 30 days unlimited. High volume, low risk. Gets bodies on the mats with zero sales pressure.
  • HeyTimmy.com Demo: The new digital funnel captures leads immediately, without the friction of "Contact Us for Pricing."
  • The Automated Close: On Day 25 of the trial, the website auto-emails the student: "Sign for a year today and we'll waive your $199 enrollment fee." The website does the selling so instructors can focus on training.
07

Dominating Local Search (E-E-A-T)

  • The Visibility Gap: Old SEO is dead. AI-driven search (Google AI Overviews) prioritizes **E-E-A-T** (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness). The new website is built to feed this engine.
  • Google Business Profile: We shift focus from generic keywords to dominating the local Map Pack by feeding Google constant, hyper-local updates, photos, and robust review responses.
  • Authentic Content Engine: Publishing real "Information Gain." Features like Student of the Month, real seminar recaps, and authentic technique breakdowns turn the website into an unassailable local authority.
  • The Concept: The website isn't a digital brochure. It is an active semantic engine capturing hyper-local intent ("Best BJJ in Renton") and routing it directly to the Kicksite free trial form.
08

The Win-Win Partnership

  • No Salary Ask: I manage the technology, the SEO, the automated funnel, and the website's performance completely on a Growth Share model.
  • The Commission Structure: I receive the student's first month's tuition as a marketing commission for every new member closed via the digital funnel. If I bring in 12 students, my membership is effectively covered—anything beyond that is pure profit for both of us.
  • The Mutual Benefit: The gym keeps 100% of the recurring MRR from month two onward, and the upfront cash flow from Annual PIFs funds immediate facility upgrades. We both win immediately.
