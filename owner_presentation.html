The Digital Evolution

A strategy to turn Instagram likes into actual bodies on the mats.

The Problem

Social Media vs. Conversions

You're posting great content on Instagram and Facebook. People see it, they get hyped, they click the link... and then they get confused. An old website is a leaky bucket. You do the hard work of getting their attention, but the website fails to close the deal.

The Solution

A High-Converting Sales Funnel

We don't just need an online brochure; we need a sales machine. The new Ruffhouse Renton site is designed to do one thing: get people to book a free trial class.

The Hero Section

"Stop Searching. Start Training."

We hit them immediately with a premium, high-energy look. No thinking required. The big red button tells them exactly what to do next.

Frictionless Onboarding

Removing The Fear

Walking into a BJJ or Muay Thai gym for the first time is terrifying for a normal person. We added a "Your First Week" section that breaks it down into 3 simple, non-threatening steps. We show them we have loaner gear and there are zero egos allowed.

Social Proof

Let Your Students Sell For You

We take your 5-star reviews and put them front and center. When a prospect sees that other beginners felt welcomed and safe, their objections disappear.

The Close

The Unified Lead Form

At the bottom of the page, there are no distractions. Just a clean form asking for their Name, Email, Phone, and Program of Interest. This goes straight to your inbox so you or the coaches can call them while they are still highly motivated.

In Conclusion

Old School Values, New School Tech

We keep the pure, hard-training essence of Ruffhouse Renton, but we package it in a way that modern consumers understand and trust. Let's get these new students on the mats.
