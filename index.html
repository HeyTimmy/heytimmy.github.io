Meet The Team

Learn from experienced practitioners dedicated to your growth.

Professor Arthur Ruff

Head Professor / Black Belt

Born in Rio de Janeiro, Arthur started Judo at four, then began Jiu Jitsu at 13 under legend Manimal (Carlson Gracie Sr. lineage). He's a multi-champion competitor who founded Ruffhouse in 1999.

Professor Andrew Ruff

Professor / Black Belt

Andrew began his Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu journey in his teens at Manimal Jiu-Jitsu. He served nearly 23 years in the US Army as a Combat Medic, dedicating his life to leadership, service, and performing under pressure.
